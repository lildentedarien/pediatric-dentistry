FAQs – Pediatric Dental Office in Darien

What age should I bring my child to the dentist?

We recommend the first visit by age 1 or when the first tooth appears.

Is your office experienced with special needs children?

Yes. We provide compassionate, personalized care for children with special healthcare needs.

How often should my child visit the dentist?

Every 6 months is ideal for preventive care unless more frequent visits are recommended.

Do you offer sedation dentistry for kids?

Yes, we offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) and other gentle sedation options for anxious children.

What makes Lil’ Dente different from a general dental clinic?
